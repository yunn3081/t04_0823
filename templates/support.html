Shipping Instructions for Frozen Cell Lines

1. Cells may be frozen either as pellets, or as a suspension in freezing media.
2. For frozen cell lines, aliquot in amounts of at least 1 million cells.
3. Place cryovials inside a small sealed plastic bag. Print the shipping form and fill it out.
4. Place form in another sealed plastic bag and include it in the shipment container.
5. Ship the materials in a polystyrene box, which is filled at least halfway with dry ice.
6. Apply Dry Ice labels on outside box. Send the package by next-day delivery service
