Who We Are?

CancerFree Biotech is a start-up company dedicated to the personalized treatment of cancer. We specialize in personalized analysis using circulating tumor cell culture to form organoid systems that help doctors and patients develop optimal treatment strategies to fight cancer at all stages and reduce the chance of ineffective drugs through bionic tumor organoid culture systems.
